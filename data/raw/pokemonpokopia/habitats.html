Habitats

In Pokémon Pokopia, in order to get Pokémon to appear in your area, you need to build a habitat for them. Each Pokémon comes from specific habitats but it isn't always a guaranteed chance as others may appear. These habitats require you to put multiple items together to create, and some time after that, a wild Pokémon may appear. The appearance of the Pokémon can vary based on location, as well as time of day and weather

Once the habitat is created and a Pokémon appears, that becomes the home for the Pokémon and you can improve it with various other elements including building items to add to it such as a bed and more.

While some Pokémon have a day or night requirement, this can be superceded by various light restrictions. Regardless of the time of day, Night exclusive Pokémon spawn will spawn if their habitat is in a place that's dark, such as a cave or underground and Day exclusive Pokémon will spawn at night if their planned habitat is surrounded by bright light.
